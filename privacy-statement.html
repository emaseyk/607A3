Privacy Statement

Haukai Restaurant collects the information necessary to ensure we know who has booked a reservation, and how we can contact you if an issue with your booking arises.
We also use cookies to collect analytical research on site visits. You will receive a pop-up to agree to cookies before this is used. The user analytics is only used to improve the site in the future.
Information you enter is not stored on our website, and is removed from our google form after it is longer relevant (your reservation date has passed).
We ensure that the information we receive from you through this webpage will not be shared with any third parties.
We will not ask you for a password, bank or credit card details. Do not give these out on our site if they are asked for.
Please contact us at 09 401 4019 if you have any further questions.
